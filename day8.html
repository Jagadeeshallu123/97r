GLENN MAXWELL

ODI T20 TEST

Glenn James Maxwell (born 14 October 1988) is an Australian professional cricketer.
He has played for the Australia national cricket team in all formats of the game since 2012
although he is primarily a One Day International and Twenty20 International specialist.
Maxwell is an all-rounder who bats right-handed and bowl off-break deliveries.
Domestically he played for Victoria and Melbourne Stars
He was part of the Australian squads that won the 2015 Cricket World Cup.
His ability to make unconventional shots like reverse sweeps and pulls.
makes it hard to set fields to cover all of his scoring areas.
